How to Play Crokinole

Objective

Be the first to reach 100 points across multiple rounds.

Setup

Each player starts every round with 12 discs. You shoot from the bottom half (blue zone); the AI shoots from the top half (pink zone). Players alternate shots — you go first.

Taking a shot

  1. Hover along the bottom baseline (the inner ring, bottom half) — a ghost disc snaps to show placement.
  2. Click and drag backward — the arrow shows where the disc will travel.
  3. Drag further back for more power. Release to flick.

The key rule

  • If the opponent has any disc on the board, your shot must make contact with at least one — otherwise your disc is immediately removed.
  • If there are no opponent discs, your disc must come to rest inside the scoring area (within the 5-point ring) or it is removed.

Round scoring

At round end, each player totals their disc points (including 20s scored during the round). The player with the higher total scores the difference. Ties score nothing.

Example: You 45 pts, AI 30 pts → you earn 15 game points.

Winning

First to 100 game points wins!
