Cyclotron

Two D-shaped electrodes ("dees") sit in a uniform magnetic field. An alternating voltage across the gap accelerates the particle on every crossing — but only if the oscillator matches the cyclotron frequency fc = qB / 2π m. In Demo the frequency is locked on; in Game you tune it yourself.
