SACE Stage 2 Physics — Electricity & Magnetism 2.5

Transformer

Two coils share a soft-iron core. An alternating voltage on the input coil drives an alternating flux through the core, and that changing flux induces an EMF in every output turn. Because each turn sees the same dΦ/dt, the voltages are simply in the ratio of the turn counts: Voutput / Vinput = Noutput / Ninput. Drag the sliders to step the voltage up or down.
