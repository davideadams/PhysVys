SACE Stage 2 Physics — Topic 2: Electromagnetism

Charged Particle Deflection

Explore how a charged particle moves in a uniform electric field between parallel plates. In Deflection mode the particle enters perpendicular to \(\mathbf{E}\) and follows a parabolic path — just like projectile motion. In Acceleration mode it enters parallel to \(\mathbf{E}\) and speeds up or slows down uniformly.
